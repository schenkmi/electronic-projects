Metallized capacitors with mixed
dielectric for stringent requirements

Ideally suited for applications with wide temperature range, e.g. automotive electronics. Almost linear TKc. Available taped and reeled.
 
Technical Data

Dielectric: Mixed film.
Capacitor electrodes: Vacuum-deposited aluminium.
Encapsulation: Flame retardent plastic case,
UL 94 V-0, with epoxy resin seal.
Colour: Red. Epoxy resin seal: Red. Marking: Black.
Temperature range: -55° C to +100° C.
Test category: 55/100/56 in accordance with IEC.
Insulation resistance at +20° C:
C < 0.33 µF: > 3 x 104 M (mean value: 1 x 105 M).
C > 0.33 µF: > 10000 sec (M x µF) (mean value: 40000 sec).
Measuring voltage: 100 V/1 min.
Dissipation factors at +20° C: tan delta
