Metallized polyester capacitors
for stringent requirements

For all standard applications in PCM > 7.5 mm. Wide capacitance range up to 100 µF and voltage ranges from 50 VDC through 2000 VDC. Low ESR. Reservoir capacitors with unlimited life expectancy even at high temperatures. Available taped and reeled up to and including case size 15 x 26 x 31.5/PCM 27.5 mm.
